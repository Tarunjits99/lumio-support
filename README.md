  ## FAQ

  **How do I pair with my partner?**
  Share your unique pairing code from the home screen. Your partner enters it on their device
   to connect.

  **How do I reset my account?**
  Sign out from the profile menu and sign back in. Contact us if you need further help.

  **Is my data private?**
  Yes. Your data is only shared between you and your paired partner. We do not sell or share
  your data with third parties.

  ## Privacy Policy

  Lumio collects minimal data required to provide the service — including your email, display
   name, and shared couple activity data. All data is stored securely using Firebase. We do
  not share your personal information with third parties.
